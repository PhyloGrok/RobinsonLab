The Team

Jeffrey Robinson, Ph.D.
PI of Robinson Lab

Dr. Robinson is bioinformatics faculty with the UMBC TLST Program, that developed the Bioinformatics track, and inovative courses incorporating the translational science paradigm. The Robinson Lab's research focuses on development of computational genomics and systems biology approaches to comparative genomics and multi-omics.
